# E_Commerce_Sales_Analysis
Analyzing the transactions of a large dataset of an online retail store using Python and Tableau to answer several key business questions, including identifying the most profitable products, understanding which countries generate the most revenue, and determining which customers contribute most significantly to sales.

Key business questions addressed:  
- Which products generate the most revenue?  
- Which countries contribute most to sales?  
- Who are the highest-value customers and what do they buy?  

## Tools Used
- **Python** – Data cleaning and analysis (Pandas, Matplotlib)  
- **Tableau** – Interactive dashboard creation

## Data Visualization (Tableau worksheet provided in folder)
**1. Top 10 Products by Revenue (Pie Chart)**  
- Highest revenue: *Paper Craft, Little Birdie* ($168,470), *Regency Cakestand Tier 3* ($142,265), *White Hanging Heart T-light Holder* ($100,392)  
- 7 out of 10 products are priced under $10, showing cheap products can generate significant revenue  

**2. Top 10 Countries by Revenue (Treemap)**  
- UK dominates with $7,285,025 in sales  
- Other top countries: Netherlands, Ireland, Germany, France, and Australia  
- Insights useful for shipping logistics, regional marketing, and international expansion  

**3. Top 15 Highest-Spending Customers (Bar Graph)**  
- High-value customers often buy products not in the top 10 by revenue  
- Most purchased items for top customers average $6 or less  
- Personalized retention strategies could increase lifetime value

## Key Insights & Recommendations
- **Inventory Management:** Ensure stock for top-performing products to avoid missed sales  
- **Customer Retention:** Implement loyalty programs or targeted promotions for high-value customers  
- **Market Expansion:** Explore opportunities in international markets, especially outside Europe  
- **Product Strategy:** Focus marketing on consistently high-revenue products

- ## Conclusion
This project demonstrates how transactional retail data can be transformed into actionable insights using Python and Tableau. Focusing on high-performing products, valuable customers, and key markets can significantly drive business growth.
